About Us

Connect 2 Farmer is a non-profit organization (NGO) that was established in November 2018. It focuses on providing a platform for individuals aged 18 and older to volunteer and support farming communities. The organization is registered under a farmer's society. The platform aims to connect people with opportunities to give back to farmers through various service activities.











Our purpose is to promote sustainable, eco-friendly living by making authentic organic products available at buyers' fingertips.
